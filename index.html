Our Donate Plan

We place special emphasis on three issues for flood victims. That is safe shelter for them, balanced diet and proper education for the child.

Food for a family

We provide balanced meals for each family of flood victims. Because they have no food.

Shelter for a family

We provide safe shelter for every family of flood victims. Because they become homeless.

Education for a child

We provide education for each child. Because schools are closed due to floods.

SERVICES

We provide good quality clothes for the needy. Especially during the two Eids for Muslims and during their religious ceremonies for followers of other religions.

We provide a variety of medicines for the needy. Because a healthy body is needed first of all. We also provide immunizations to children.

If they are affected by floods or any other reason, we also pay a good amount of money to compensate them. For this, I give more priority to zakat money.
